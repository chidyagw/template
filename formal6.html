This author shows that the future of gathering information and advancing in the field of engineering is strongly embedded in the use of research. Technology is also key in all the findings made by these articles. Most of the experiments in the field of engineering require high technology equipment in order for the necessary experiments to be carried out as in the example given in the second article of the observations made by the scientists of how certain drugs affect the blood capillary system. This requires a good amount of technology and this shows how the engineering field cannot advance without the use of high technology. In the study of proteins, protein models are made using advanced computer programming. This allows the scientists to study the behavior of proteins much easier which is something they would not be able to do if it wasn’t for the role played by technology. I talked to Dr. Ron Averill who is a professor in the department of mechanical engineering. He emphasized the role been played by technology and research in the field of engineering. He has been part of a group employed by Bill Gates to make containers to carry vaccines to remote places in Africa. The process involved researching about the places they were going to carry the vaccines. This included investigating about the maximum and minimum temperatures in the areas. Advanced technology was then used in the designing of the containers and this proved to me the importance of advanced technology in the engineering discipline and since engineers are the one responsible for the developing and designing of cutting edge technology it becomes so obvious that this field cannot survive without technology. I have also talked to Charlene Chabata a student in Brown University who is currently involved in a research project in protein engineering. The purpose of the project she is working on is to make the e-coli bacteria in the stomach to release proteins that can be used to make medicine to cure people. According to her a lot of research has been done in this area. She also emphasizes the importance of deep research and the use of high advanced technology in the projects she is doing. They make use of technology like Mat lab to develop software prototypes of the kind of protein they want to come up with and also they make use of 3d printers to then bring the software model into reality. This also emphasizes the use of advanced technology in engineering. The use of exact and accurate calculations is also important which can be shown in the form of graphs.
